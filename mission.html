Our Mission

At Mountain of Fire and Miracle Ministry, our mission is to be an instrument of God's power, purpose, and presence in the lives of people across the world. We are committed to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ with power, demonstrating God's deliverance and healing through prayer, intercession, and the application of God's Word.

We believe in a Spirit-filled church that operates in the supernatural power of God, breaking chains of bondage, delivering the oppressed, healing the sick, and transforming lives through faith in Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To establish a global movement of Spirit-filled believers who walk in the power and authority of Jesus Christ, operating in signs, wonders, and miracles, bringing hope and restoration to the lost and broken.

We envision Mountain of Fire and Miracle Ministry as a beacon of light in dark places, where the sick are healed, the bound are loosed, the lost are found, and the dead are raised through the mighty power of God.

Our Core Values

Faith in God: Unwavering trust and belief in God's Word, promises, and power to transform lives and situations.

Deliverance: Actively ministering freedom from spiritual bondage, demonic oppression, and generational curses.

Healing & Miracles: Manifesting God's healing power and performing miracles as evidence of His kingdom and love.

Prayer & Intercession: Engaging in powerful prayer and intercession as the foundation of all spiritual work and transformation.
