A House of Prayer

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries is known for prayer that is focused, loud, specific, and grounded in the Word of God. The pattern is simple: praise God, confess the Word, pray with fire, reject every work of darkness, and close with thanksgiving.

Pray with faith. Speak clearly. Stay alert. End every session with gratitude.

Mode of Prayer

  1. Begin with praise and worship.
  2. Declare Bible verses over your life.
  3. Pray specific prayers with holy boldness.
  4. Reject evil patterns, fear, and delay.
  5. Close with thanksgiving and joy.

Prayer Rhythm

  • Pray with focus, not distraction.
  • Repeat key declarations with faith.
  • Fast when led, and stay disciplined.
  • Use the Bible as your foundation.
Say it aloud. Believe it fully. Receive it by faith.

Scriptural Anchor

"The effectual fervent prayer of a righteous man availeth much" James 5:16. MFM prayer keeps the heart on fire, the mouth active, and the spirit alert.

Prayer Points

Pray each point with confidence, repeating it as your spirit leads.

  • Father, let the fire of the Holy Ghost consume every weakness in my prayer life.
  • Every power troubling my peace, be silenced by the blood of Jesus.
  • I receive grace to pray with power, purity, and consistency.
  • Every door of blessing assigned to me, open by the mercy of God.
  • My life, my home, and my future shall glorify the name of Jesus.
Declare: I am planted in prayer, strengthened by the Word, and covered by the blood of Jesus.

Closing Charge

Keep praying until your heart is settled in faith. Keep declaring the Word until your spirit is aligned with God’s promises. Keep giving thanks, because the Lord who hears prayer is faithful to answer.
