By the grace of God, the Mountain of Fire & Miracles Ministries is led by our Father-in-the-Lord, Dr. Daniel Kolawole Olukoya. Furthermore, the smooth running of the church owes its success to the administrative responsibilities of seven Assistant General Overseers (AGOs). They are;

  1. Pastor Kehinde Adegbolahan
  2. Pastor Gbesan Adebambo
  3. Pastor Timothy Akano
  4. Pastor Abiodun Ladejola
  5. Pastor Lawrence Olaseinde
  6. Pastor Felix Peters
  7. Pastor Femi Ajila
The Assistant General Overseers (AGOs) of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) manage specific global departments, directorates, and foreign regional blocks rather than localized single territories, which are instead handled by Regional Overseers.The portfolios and specific departmental responsibilities assigned to the AGOs include:

AGO Portfolios and Departments

Pastor Kehinde Adegbolahan: Serves as the AGO in charge of Missions and Evangelism. He coordinates church plantings, outreach efforts, and evangelical missions globally.

Pastor Gbesan Adebambo: Appointed as the AGO for Administration. He oversees the day-to-day administrative operations, structural policies, and coordination of the church worldwide.

Pastor Abiodun Ladejola: Serves as the AGO in charge of Training and Research. He directs minister training, spiritual workforce development, educational research, and doctrine materials. (Note: He also steps in to lead major global tasks like Missions and Evangelism when designated by leadership).

Pastor Lawrence Olaseinde: Functions as the AGO overseeing African Countries. He manages the growth, administration, and oversight of all MFM branches outside Nigeria across the African continent.

Pastor Timothy Akano: Serves as the AGO for Europe and Special Duties. He coordinates all MFM branches across European territories (including directly superintending MFM Valencia, Spain) alongside temporary assignments.

Pastor Felix Peters & Pastor Femi Ajila: These AGOs handle key core directorates at the MFM International Headquarters and the MFM Prayer City complex. They oversee specialized operations, youth development frameworks, infrastructural management, and major international convention logistics.MFM also has a large number of senior pastors and ministers who cater for the spiritual needs of the ever growing church.
